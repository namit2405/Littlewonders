About Little Wonders Early Learning Centre

Little Wonders ELC is a warm, nurturing early learning centre where every child is encouraged to explore, grow, and shine with confidence. Our thoughtfully designed, play-based programs focus on building strong foundations in learning, creativity, and social development. With a safe and stimulating environment, along with caring and experienced educators, we support each child’s unique journey—helping them develop essential life skills, independence, and a lifelong love for learning. – it is the new flagship centre and a culmination of over 25 years of experience. Little Wonders Early Learning is an exciting new education and wellbeing model that brings all the best ideas, philosophies and experience into one place..
